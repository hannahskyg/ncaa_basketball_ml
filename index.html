Project Overview

This project focuses on the "Cinderella" phenomenon in the NCAA Division I Men's Basketball Tournament, otherwise known as March Madness. A Cinderella is a team seeded 10th or lower that advances to the Sweet Sixteen or further. Using historical regular-season data, we've created an anomaly detection classifier to identify teams that demonstrated the "Giant-Killer Footprint," a specific statistical profile that involves efficiency, three-point volume, and pace that deviate from typical early-exit mid-majors.
